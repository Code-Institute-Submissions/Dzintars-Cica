Little About Me

Hi, my name is Dzintars Cica, originaly I'm from Latvia, but now I live in UK, London. I'm born in 1993 28th January in Talsi, Latvia. I'm profesional singer. I do enjoy singing, I always put my soul in my songs and trying always to shear a piece of it with my listeners.

My Career

Started my singer career in age 5. When my parents took me to music school, where my talent was spoted. I fall in love with singing and music in general. I also learned to play piano in which I'm realy good. As a kid I participated in lot of concerts local and neighbor countries. I reliesed my first album "Rich Gipsy"

©copyright Photo from CD cover "Rich Gipsy"

In age 10, I won Latvian kids eurovision. I had chance to sing in kids worlds eurovision in Copenhagen 2003,which i finished in 9th place. After which my popularity gained new level. I started to have a lot of invitations for singing gigs, also county where, I live gave me piano as a gift,and I was presented as vocal of future.

©copyright Photo from Copenhagen 2003

As everething progrest, I got older and every year my popularity and singing skills was growing with me. Every year in Latvia I have my concert tour in all bigest cities. My songs are played on radio daily.

©copyright Photo from my tour in Latvia for mothers day

With years, I began very strong figure in Latvian music industry. I won Latvian music award in 2016 with my song "Vijolnieks" and 2017 with my original song "Liktenlinijas". By this time, I had three my original songs, two awards, every year my solo concert tour, and many many smaller gigs.

©copyright Photo from wining Latvian singing award 2017

With time me and my manager we decided to try something new. I and another three guys we created singing band "Ciganzeni" translation: "Gypy Boys" Each of us was very known singer in Latvia, and when we started our group, it gone viral in Latvia, we was very popular, we had couple month tour in Latvia.
